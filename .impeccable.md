## Design Context

### Users
Administrative staff in a public hospital environment. They use the app during operational work to find contact information quickly, confirm sensitive phone details safely, and maintain records without depending on internet connectivity.

### Brand Personality
Calm, sober, legible.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme. Clinical and restrained rather than decorative. Easy to interpret at a glance. Prioritize hierarchy, spacing, and clarity over visual flourish. Avoid repeated information, oversized decorative containers, and heavy visual accents.

### Design Principles
1. Make scanning faster than reading.
2. Show each piece of information once, in the most useful place.
3. Keep controls compact, stable, and visually proportional.
4. Use contrast and spacing for hierarchy, not decoration.
5. Preserve accessibility and predictability under dense operational use.
